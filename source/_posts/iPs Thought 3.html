Perhaps more profound that iPhone Notes past, today's quote attempts to generalize all actions of humans in general.

Time: June 9th 2015
Time: 8:29 AM
Place: Kitchen
"Everyone's actions are driven by the pursuit of the next state of mental comfort."

True selflessness is a very rare trait. What does one gain from going out of their way to help another? An intrinsic sense of fufilment is what at least I'm after when I do things that only benefit others, and there in lies the crux of this thought. While I'm not privy to anything tangible as a result of selflessness, the satisfaction I receive is payment. The mental comfort conjured by such actions can be equated to real world objects, gestures or actions.

So to re-visit the thought, mental comfort is what drives everyone. Mental comfort through money earned, family cared for, or even urges sated. A human's actions can be digressed back to mending the current lull in mental comfort.
